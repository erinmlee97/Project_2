Escaping 2020 on Earth

After experiencing the whirlwind that has been the year 2020, our group has decided to search for an alternative home. Through the examination of planets, species, and climates, as well as an analysis of spacecraft and vehicles, we will determine the best place to relocate.

Jedis: Allred, Azar, Lee, and Swierczynski
